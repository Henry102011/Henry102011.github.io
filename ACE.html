About Academic Community Engagement

Academic Community Engagement (ACE) at Sam Houston State University is a transformative program that connects classroom learning with community service. Through ACE, ITSA members apply their technical skills to address real community needs while gaining valuable experience and developing a sense of civic responsibility.

The ACE program at SHSU is designed to create mutually beneficial partnerships between students and community organizations, fostering meaningful relationships that enhance both educational experiences and community development.

ITSA's Current ACE Projects

Our Information Technology Student Association is actively involved in several ACE initiatives that allow our members to apply their technical skills to benefit the community:

  • Digital Literacy Workshops - Teaching basic computer skills to underserved populations
  • Nonprofit Website Development - Creating and maintaining websites for local nonprofit organizations
  • Technology Recycling Program - Refurbishing donated computers for schools and community centers
  • Cybersecurity Awareness Training - Educating community members about online safety
  • App Development for Community Needs - Building mobile applications to address local challenges
