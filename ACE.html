What is ACE?

Academic Community Engagement (ACE) is a program designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and community service. Through ACE, students apply their classroom knowledge to real-world challenges, working with community partners to create meaningful solutions while developing professional skills and civic responsibility.

Our Mission

The ACE Project aims to foster meaningful connections between educational institutions and their surrounding communities. By engaging students in service-learning opportunities, we create a mutually beneficial relationship where students gain practical experience while communities receive valuable assistance and resources.

Key Components

Service Learning: Integrating community service with academic instruction to enrich learning experiences.

Community Partnerships: Building sustainable relationships with local organizations and businesses.

Student Development: Enhancing students' professional skills, leadership abilities, and civic engagement.

Social Impact: Addressing community needs through collaborative projects and initiatives.

Get Involved

There are numerous ways to participate in the ACE program:

  • Join an existing ACE project
  • Propose a new community partnership
  • Volunteer for community service events
  • Integrate ACE components into coursework
  • Attend ACE workshops and training sessions
