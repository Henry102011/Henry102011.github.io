About ITSA

Who We Are

The Information Technology Student Association (ITSA) is dedicated to providing students with opportunities to develop their technical skills, professional network, and career readiness. We organize workshops, hackathons, industry tours, and social events to create a vibrant community of technology enthusiasts.

We are an organization that helps with social networking and events in the field of IT by bringing students closer to business professionals and preparing them for after school by talking with guest speakers, reviewing for certifications, and going to conferences to bolster the readiness of students for a successful life in the IT world.

Current Funding Request: USITCC Conference 2023

U.S. Information Technology Collegiate Conference (USITCC/ISECON 2023)

Date: March 30th to April 1st, 2023

Location: 7121 Bishop Plano, TX

Funding Needed: $3,150 for 6 attendees (4 club officers and 2 additional members)

About the Event

From the USITCC website: "The USITCC is produced by the Foundation for IT Education as part of its ongoing efforts to fulfill its mission of promoting information technology research and education and to encourage and support individuals entering and considering entering the information technology profession. The Foundation offers scholarships to students enrolled in undergraduate collegiate information technology-related programs across America."

The Event is in Plano, Texas and hosts a variety of competitions for students including Database, System Analysis and Design, and PC Troubleshooting. There will also be guest speakers and industry professionals to network with and learn from through hands-on learning and exercises.

Why This Matters

This event is a significant opportunity for students to get hands-on experience with projects and connect with professionals in the field. It will allow students to gain a deeper understanding of the information technology field and interact with professionals in a work environment. Students will learn how to better communicate in the business world and network with various people and companies attending the event.
