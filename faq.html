Common Questions

What is ITSA?

+

The Information Technology Student Association (ITSA) is a student-run organization dedicated to providing opportunities for students interested in technology. We organize workshops, networking events, hackathons, and other activities to help members develop technical skills and professional connections.

How do I become a member?

+

You can become a member by filling out our registration form. Membership is open to all students interested in information technology, regardless of major or experience level.

What are the benefits of joining ITSA?

+

Benefits include:

  • Access to exclusive workshops and training sessions
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Participation in hackathons and coding competitions
  • Resume building through collaborative projects
  • Mentorship from senior students and faculty
  • Job and internship opportunities through our industry partners

How much does membership cost?

+

There is no membership cost to join.

Do I need to be a Computer Science or IT major to join?

+

No, ITSA welcomes students from all majors who are interested in technology. Whether you're studying business, arts, engineering, or any other field, if you're interested in technology, you're welcome to join!

How often does ITSA meet?

+

We hold general meetings twice a month, typically on Wednesday evenings. Additionally, we organize various workshops, social events, and project meetings throughout the semester. Our event calendar is regularly updated on our website.
